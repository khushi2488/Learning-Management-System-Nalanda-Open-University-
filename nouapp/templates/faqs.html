Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to commonly asked questions and troubleshooting guides

Admission Related

What are the eligibility criteria for admission? +
The eligibility criteria vary by program. Generally, for undergraduate programs, you need to have completed 10+2 from a recognized board. For postgraduate programs, a bachelor's degree in a relevant field is required. Please check the specific program requirements on our courses page.
How do I apply for admission? +
You can apply online through our portal. Click on the "Register" button, fill in your details, select your desired program, upload required documents, and submit the application form. You'll receive a confirmation email once your application is submitted.
When does the admission process start? +
Admissions typically open twice a year - for the January session (applications from October to December) and the July session (applications from April to June). Please check our announcements page for exact dates.

Courses & Study Materials

What courses are available? +
We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines including Arts, Science, Commerce, Management, and Computer Applications. Visit our courses section for a complete list of available programs.
How do I access study materials? +
After logging into your student portal, navigate to the "Study Materials" section. All course materials, notes, and resources are available for download. Materials are organized by program, branch, and year for easy access.
Are the courses recognized? +
Yes, all our courses are recognized by UGC-DEB (University Grants Commission - Distance Education Bureau) and are valid for higher education and employment purposes across India.

Technical Support

I forgot my password. What should I do? +
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email ID or user ID, and you'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password.
Why can't I download study materials? +
Make sure you're logged in with valid credentials. Check your internet connection and ensure pop-ups are not blocked in your browser. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or contact technical support.
Which browsers are supported? +
Our portal works best on the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

Fees & Payment

What are the course fees? +
Course fees vary depending on the program you choose. Please refer to the fee structure available on our website or contact our admission office for detailed information about specific program fees.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept online payments through credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets. You can also pay through demand draft in favor of "Nalanda Open University" payable at Patna.
Is there any refund policy? +
Refund requests are considered on a case-by-case basis within 15 days of payment. A processing fee may be deducted. Please refer to our refund policy document or contact the accounts department for detailed information.

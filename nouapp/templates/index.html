ABOUT NOU e-GYAN PORTAL



Nalanda Open University welcomes all our students, faculties & study centres to the very new & innovative online study portal NOU e-Gyan. The portal is a unique initiative by Hon’ble Vice-Chancellor Prof. (Dr.) K C Sinha, which is aimed at providing online study material to the students of NOU.

The portal is accessible from any nook and corner of the world if the system allows permission to its users. It is a web-based solution assisting 24×7 to its authorized users. It will resolve the academic issues such as e-Content Distribution, Delivery, Tracking, Assessment, Progress Monitoring & Controlling of all stakeholders of the system i.e. students, teachers, study centre administrators as well as Nalanda Open University Admins when they are at a distance.
